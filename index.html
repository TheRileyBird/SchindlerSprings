A Family Tradition Since 1918

Schindler Spring Company is a custom spring manufacturer located in Hammond, Louisiana. We manufacture compression springs, torsion springs, extension springs, power springs, wire forms, spring washers, and many other metal components. We cold-coil springs with material ranging from .005" to .625". Our specialty is short orders and rush deliveries. Call us for a quote or for more information on our products and services.
