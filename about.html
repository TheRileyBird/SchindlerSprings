A Family Tradition Since 1918

In 1918, H.P. Schindler began a family tradition well renowned throughout the South. After 65 years and three generations, the Schindler family is now dedicated solely to the advancement of that tradition -- precision spring manufacturing.

As a leading spring manufacturer in the South, the unique product line of Schindler Spring Company, Inc. produces cold wound springs ranges from .005" diameter to 5/8" diameter material and hot wound springs from 5/8" diameter to 1 1/2 diameter material. Additionally, many types of flat formed and speciality types are produced. A full range of sizes and materials are stocked, plus the expert talent of our "spring makers" enable us to immediately process your order.

Schindler springs are in use in thousands of industries and applications. The knowledge gained from this experience is reflected in our quality springs and personalized service extended to each customer. Quality and Service -- at Schindler Spring Company, Inc., we take pride in providing our customers with the best of both.
